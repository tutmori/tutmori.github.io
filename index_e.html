■ About the Special Reconstruction Income Tax

On November 30 2011, the Japanese government approved the bill for taking Special Measures for Securing Financial Resources Necessary for Reconstruction from the Great East Japan Earthquake.   As a part of these measures, a tax called the Special Reconstruction Income Tax has now been introduced, in which a surtax of 2.1% of the statutory income tax rate will apply to all incomes (including royalties income) payable in Japan on or after January 1 2013, and for 25 years thereafter.
